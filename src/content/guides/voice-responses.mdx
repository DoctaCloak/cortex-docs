# Voice Responses

Cortex can speak AI responses aloud using ElevenLabs text-to-speech. This is useful for hands-free review sessions, accessibility, or when you prefer listening to reading.

<Callout type="info">
Voice responses are available on Student ($5/mo) and above. See [Pricing Tiers](/docs/core-concepts/tiers) for quotas.
</Callout>

## Enabling Voice

In the AI Assistant, toggle the voice icon to enable spoken responses. The AI will both display text and read the response aloud.

## Voice Models

| Model | Latency | Quality | Cost |
|-------|---------|---------|------|
| Flash | ~200ms | Good | $0.08/1K chars |
| Turbo | ~300ms | Better | $0.11/1K chars |

Cortex uses **Flash** by default for speed. Turbo is used for longer, synthesized responses where quality matters more.

## Usage Tracking

Voice responses count against your monthly quota:

- **Student**: 15 voice responses/month
- **Pro**: 60 voice responses/month
- **Power**: 200 voice responses/month

Check your remaining quota on the dashboard or via the `/api/quota` endpoint.
